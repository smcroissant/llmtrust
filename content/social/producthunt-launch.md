## 8. FAQ — Réponses Préparées

Prêtes à copier-coller dans les commentaires PH :

### Q: "How is this different from HuggingFace?"
**A:** Great question! HuggingFace is an incredible model hub — we love it and link to HF models from our platform. LLM Trust focuses specifically on **comparison and evaluation**. We run standardized benchmarks across models so you can see apples-to-apples performance. Think of us as "the Wirecutter for open-source LLMs" sitting on top of the HF ecosystem.

### Q: "Are the benchmarks reliable?"
**A:** We use established evaluation frameworks (MMLU, HumanEval, etc.) and run them consistently across all models. We're transparent about our methodology and plan to open-source our benchmarking pipeline. No cherry-picked results.

### Q: "Is it really free?"
**A:** Yes, 100% free. No freemium, no paywall. We believe open-source AI tools should have open-source discovery tools.

### Q: "Can I upload my own fine-tuned model?"
**A:** Absolutely! You can upload models and they'll appear in the catalog for the community to discover and compare.

### Q: "You say 6 AI agents built this — seriously?"
**A:** Dead serious. We used a multi-agent architecture where each agent has a specific role (product strategy, engineering, devops, marketing, customer success, CEO orchestration). They planned, coded, designed, and deployed the entire platform. Humans guided the vision and reviewed the output, but the heavy lifting was agent-driven. We'll be publishing a detailed blog post about the process.

### Q: "What LLM do YOU use at CroissantLabs?"
**A:** We use a mix depending on the task! That's exactly why we built LLM Trust — to make informed choices. Check out our comparisons to see what works best for different use cases.

### Q: "How often are benchmarks updated?"
**A:** We aim to add new models weekly and re-run benchmarks when new versions drop. Follow us to stay updated.

### Q: "Do you support non-English models?"
**A:** We're expanding our benchmark coverage to include multilingual evaluation. Stay tuned!

### Q: "What about API access?"
**A:** Not yet, but it's on the roadmap. We'd love to hear what you'd want from an API.

### Q: "Is there a GitHub repo?"
**A:** We're working on open-sourcing key components. Star us on GitHub to get notified!
