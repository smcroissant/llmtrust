## 4. Réponses aux Commentaires PH (Q&R Pré-Écrites)

> **Usage:** Copier-coller, adapter légèrement si besoin. Toujours répondre dans les 30 min max.

---

**Q1: "How is this different from HuggingFace?"**

> Great question! HuggingFace is an incredible model hub — we love it and link to HF models from our platform. LLM Trust focuses specifically on **comparison and evaluation**. We run standardized benchmarks across models so you can see apples-to-apples performance. Think of us as "the Wirecutter for open-source LLMs" sitting on top of the HF ecosystem. 🙏

---

**Q2: "Are the benchmarks reliable?"**

> We use established evaluation frameworks (MMLU, HumanEval, MT-Bench, etc.) and run them consistently across all models. We're fully transparent about our methodology — no cherry-picked results. We're also working on open-sourcing our benchmarking pipeline so the community can verify and contribute! 📊

---

**Q3: "Built by 6 AI agents — seriously?"**

> Dead serious! We used a multi-agent architecture where each agent has a specific role (product strategy, engineering, devops, marketing, customer success, CEO orchestration). They planned, coded, designed, and deployed the entire platform. Humans guided the vision and reviewed output, but the heavy lifting was agent-driven. We'll publish a detailed blog post about the process soon! 🤖

---

**Q4: "Is it really free? No freemium?"**

> Yes, 100% free. No freemium, no paywall, no hidden pricing tiers. We believe open-source AI tools should have open-source discovery tools. We might add optional premium features in the future (API access, enterprise dashboards) but the core comparison platform will always be free. 🔓

---

**Q5: "Can I upload my own fine-tuned model?"**

> Absolutely! You can upload your models and they'll appear in the catalog for the community to discover and compare. Just create an account and hit "Upload Model." We support HuggingFace model links and direct uploads. 📤

---

**Q6: "How does this compare to LMSYS Chatbot Arena?"**

> Great comparison! LMSYS does chat-based Elo rankings (which we love). LLM Trust focuses on standardized benchmarks (MMLU, HumanEval, etc.) with more granular metrics — pricing, hardware requirements, context windows. Different approaches: LMSYS = "which model feels better to chat with" and LLM Trust = "which model performs better on specific tasks." Complementary! ⚖️

---

**Q7: "Do you have an API?"**

> Not yet, but it's high on our roadmap! We'd love to hear what you'd want from an API — model metadata, benchmark results, comparison endpoints? Drop us a comment or email and we'll prioritize based on demand. 🔌

---

**Q8: "How often are benchmarks updated?"**

> We aim to add new models weekly and re-run benchmarks when significant new versions drop. Our goal is to be the most up-to-date comparison platform for open-source LLMs. Follow us to stay in the loop! 🔄

---

**Q9: "What about multilingual / non-English models?"**

> We're expanding our benchmark coverage to include multilingual evaluation. It's on the roadmap for Q2 2026. If you have specific language benchmarks you'd like to see, let us know! 🌍

---

**Q10: "Is there a GitHub repo? Can I contribute?"**

> We're working on open-sourcing key components (especially the benchmarking pipeline). Star us on GitHub to get notified when we drop it! We'd love community contributions — especially around new benchmarks and model evaluations. ⭐

---

**Bonus — Q11: "What LLM do YOU use at CroissantLabs?"**

> We use a mix depending on the task — that's exactly why we built LLM Trust! For code generation, DeepSeek Coder is strong. For general tasks, Mistral and Llama variants. Check out our comparisons to see what works best for different use cases. 😄
