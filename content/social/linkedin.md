## Post 2 — Insight / Thought Leadership

```
Your engineering team is probably overspending on LLMs by 5-10x.

Here's why:

Most teams default to GPT-4 for everything. It's the "safe" choice.

But after benchmarking 100+ models, here's what we found:

→ 70% of production LLM tasks don't need GPT-4
→ A well-chosen open source model can match GPT-3.5 at 1/10th the cost
→ The "best" model varies wildly by task

Real example from our data:

Task: Extracting structured data from documents
• GPT-4: $30/1M tokens, 95% accuracy
• Llama 3 8B: $0.10/1M tokens, 91% accuracy
• Mistral 7B: $0.15/1M tokens, 89% accuracy

For most teams, 91% accuracy at 300x lower cost is the obvious choice.

The skill in 2025 isn't using AI. It's choosing the RIGHT AI for each task.

We built LLM Trust to make that decision easier → llmtrust.com

How does your team approach model selection?

#AI #Engineering #CostOptimization #LLM #MLOps
```

## Post 3 — Behind the scenes / Build in public

```
What I learned cataloging 100+ LLMs:

1. The model landscape is moving faster than anyone can track. We've had 30+ significant releases in the last 3 months alone.

2. Benchmarks are necessary but insufficient. A model that scores 90% on MMLU might fail miserably on your specific use case.

3. Price is the most underrated factor. Teams focus on capability and ignore that they could run 5 models for the price of 1 GPT-4 call.

4. Open source isn't just "catching up" — in some categories (code, math, multilingual), it's already ahead.

5. Context window size is overrated. Most real-world use cases don't need 200K tokens. A good 32K model with proper chunking outperforms a bad 200K model.

Building LLM Trust has been a masterclass in the current state of AI. Happy to share more insights if people are interested.

What's your biggest surprise from the LLM ecosystem?

→ llmtrust.com if you want to explore the data yourself

#AI #OpenSource #MachineLearning #BuildInPublic
```

## Strategy d'engagement LinkedIn

### Commenter stratégiquement

- **CTO posts** → Add technical insight, mention model selection challenges
- **AI/ML thought leaders** → Agree and add data point from LLM Trust
- **"We just shipped AI feature"** → Congrats + ask which models they evaluated
- **AI cost discussions** → Share pricing insights from the platform

### Format gagnants

- **Hook dans les 2 lignes** — LinkedIn coupe après ~3 lignes
- **Une idée par ligne** — Easy to scan
- **Pas de markdown** — Just line breaks
- **CTA clair** — Question ouverte à la fin
- **3-5 hashtags max** — À la fin du post

### Growth hack

- Engager sur les posts de gros comptes dans les 30 premières minutes
- Répondre aux commentaires de ton post dans l'heure
- Taguer 2-3 personnes pertinentes dans les commentaires (pas dans le post)
- Reposter le même contenu avec un angle différent après 2 semaines
