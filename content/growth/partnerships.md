> **Owner:** Aura (Head of Marketing & Growth, CroissantLabs)

> **Objectif:** Construire un réseau de partenaires stratégiques pour accélérer l'adoption de LLM Trust comme référence des benchmarks LLM indépendants.

---

## Vision Partenariats

LLM Trust est **neutre** — on ne vend pas de modèles, on les évalue. C'est notre super-pouvoir partenarial : nous sommes le **"label de confiance"** que chaque acteur de l'écosystème LLM a intérêt à promouvoir.

**Règle d'or du pitch :** Chaque partenaire potentiel a un problème que LLM Trust résout — on identifie CE problème et on propose une solution concrète.

## 5. Email Templates d'Approche

### Template A — Partenaires Modèles (Meta, Mistral, Google, DeepSeek, Qwen)

```
Subject: LLM Trust × [COMPANY] — Independent benchmarks your community deserves

Hi [NAME],

I'm [YOUR NAME], Head of Growth at LLM Trust (llmtrust.ai) — an independent 
benchmarking platform for open-source LLMs.

We've been running comprehensive evaluations on [MODEL_NAME] and the results 
are impressive — especially [SPECIFIC_METRIC where they shine, e.g., "coding 
benchmarks where Llama 4 scores top-3"].

Why I'm reaching out:

We believe [COMPANY]'s models deserve transparent, third-party validation — 
not marketing claims, but real numbers that developers trust. That's exactly 
what LLM Trust provides.

What we propose:
• Official inclusion of [MODEL_NAME] in our benchmark suite with [SPECIFIC_CATEGORIES]
• Cross-promotion: our [X] monthly visitors see your results; your community 
  gets a credible reference
• Optional: co-authored analysis piece on [RELEVANT_TOPIC, e.g., "multilingual 
  performance of open models"]

We're already benchmarking your models (transparency is our thing). We'd love 
to do it officially, with your input on evaluation priorities.

15 minutes to discuss? I'll adapt to your schedule.

Best,
[YOUR NAME]
Head of Growth, LLM Trust
llmtrust.ai | [YOUR TWITTER/LINKEDIN]
```
