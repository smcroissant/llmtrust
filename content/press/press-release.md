## CroissantLabs Launches LLM Trust, the First Discovery Platform Built for Open-Source LLMs

**Paris, France — March 2026** — CroissantLabs, the AI-native startup operated by six specialized AI agents, today announced the launch of **LLM Trust**, a free discovery and evaluation platform designed to help developers and teams navigate the rapidly expanding world of open-source Large Language Models.

---

### The Challenge

The open-source LLM landscape has exploded. With new models launching weekly — from Mistral, Meta, Google, and dozens of independent labs — developers face a paradox of choice. Finding the right model requires cross-referencing benchmarks, reading fragmented community discussions, and manually evaluating licensing terms.

### The Solution

LLM Trust consolidates this scattered ecosystem into a single, trusted platform:

- **150+ models cataloged** with detailed technical profiles
- **Side-by-side comparisons** on performance, cost efficiency, and licensing
- **Community trust scores** from developers running models in production
- **Weekly updates** to keep pace with the fast-moving ecosystem

> « We built LLM Trust because choosing an open-source LLM shouldn't require a PhD in ML research and a weekend of reading Reddit threads. It should take five minutes. »
>
> — **Samuel Mamane**, Founder & CEO, CroissantLabs

### How It Works

LLM Trust aggregates publicly available benchmarks (MMLU, HumanEval, MT-Bench, and more), combines them with community-reported real-world performance data, and presents everything through an intuitive interface. Developers can filter by model size, license type, hardware requirements, and specific capabilities.

### Availability

LLM Trust is available today at **llmtrust.ai**. The platform offers a free tier for individual developers and Pro plans for teams requiring advanced comparison features and API access.

### About CroissantLabs

CroissantLabs is a Paris-based AI-native startup building developer tools for the AI era. The company operates with a unique structure — six specialized AI agents handle product, engineering, DevOps, customer success, and marketing — enabling rapid iteration and lean operations. LLM Trust is the company's flagship product.
