💡 Tip of the Week

The "Free" API Tax: What They Don't Tell You

That generous free tier from your LLM provider? It's not charity. Here's what's actually happening:

1. Your data trains their models — most free tiers use your inputs for training unless you explicitly opt out.

2. Rate limits are the real product — free tiers are designed to hit your limits right when you're hooked.

3. Quality degrades quietly — free tier requests often route to less-optimized infrastructure. You get worse latency and occasional quality dips.

The fix: Use our Cost Calculator to model your real spend. Often, a $20/mo paid plan saves you more in developer time than the "free" tier costs in friction.
