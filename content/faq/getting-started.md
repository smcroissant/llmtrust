# Getting Started FAQ

## What is LLM Trust?

LLM Trust is a centralized platform designed to help developers, researchers, and AI enthusiasts discover, compare, and evaluate large language models. We provide verified model information, performance benchmarks, detailed technical specifications, and community-driven reviews — all in one place. Our mission is to bring transparency and trust to the rapidly growing ecosystem of open-source and commercial LLMs.

Whether you're choosing your first model to run locally or evaluating options for a production deployment, LLM Trust gives you the data and context you need to make informed decisions. We aggregate information from multiple sources, verify it against official documentation, and present it in a way that's accessible to both beginners and experts.

## How do I browse models?

Browsing models on LLM Trust is straightforward. From the homepage, you can explore our curated collections of models organized by category, capability, and popularity. Use the search bar to find specific models by name, architecture, or use case. You can also apply filters to narrow down results by parameter count, context length, license type, file format, and more.

Each model has a dedicated detail page where you'll find comprehensive information including description, architecture details, available quantizations, download links, benchmark scores, and community reviews. You can sort and filter the model list based on your specific needs — for example, filtering to show only models that run on your hardware or support a particular language.

## Do I need an account?

No, you do not need an account to browse models, read reviews, or access most of the information on LLM Trust. Our platform is designed to be open and accessible to everyone. You can freely explore model listings, compare specifications, and read our technical guides without signing up.

However, creating a free account unlocks additional features such as saving favorite models to watchlists, leaving reviews and ratings, participating in community discussions, and receiving personalized recommendations based on your interests. For advanced features like API access and advanced analytics, a Pro subscription is required.

## Is it free?

LLM Trust offers a robust free tier that includes access to our complete model directory, detailed model pages, search and filtering tools, community reviews, and all of our educational content including this FAQ and technical documentation. The free experience is designed to be genuinely useful without any paywalls on essential information.

We also offer a Pro subscription that unlocks additional features such as advanced benchmark comparisons, API access for programmatic queries, priority model verification, and early access to new features. Pro is aimed at teams and professionals who need deeper analytics and integration capabilities. Individual developers and hobbyists will find the free tier more than sufficient for their needs.

## How do I compare models?

LLM Trust provides a dedicated comparison tool that lets you evaluate two or more models side by side. To use it, navigate to any model page and click the "Compare" button, or select multiple models from the browse page. The comparison view displays key attributes in parallel, including parameter count, context length, license, supported quantizations, and benchmark performance.

Beyond raw numbers, our comparison tool highlights relative strengths and trade-offs. You'll see visual indicators showing which model excels in specific areas like inference speed, memory efficiency, coding ability, or multilingual support. This makes it easy to understand not just what differs between models, but why those differences matter for your specific use case.
