# Troubleshooting FAQ

## Model won't download

If a model download fails or stalls, there are several common causes and solutions to try. First, check your internet connection stability — large model files (multi-gigabyte) require a stable connection and may timeout on slow or intermittent networks. If you're on a metered or restricted connection, the download may be blocked. Try switching to a different network or using a download manager that supports resume functionality.

Second, the hosting platform (usually Hugging Face) may be experiencing high traffic or temporary outages. Check the platform's status page and try again after a few minutes. Some models are hosted behind rate limits that cap download speeds or frequency — waiting a few minutes and retrying often resolves this. If the download link on LLM Trust appears broken or points to a removed model, please report it using the "Report Issue" button on the model page so we can update the listing.

## Search not working

If the search function isn't returning expected results, start by simplifying your query. Try searching for a single keyword rather than a complex phrase, and check for typos in model names. Our search supports partial matching, so "llama" will find "Llama 2" and "Llama 3" models. If you're using filters in combination with search, try removing some filters — overly restrictive combinations may return no results even when matching models exist.

Clear your browser cache and cookies if the search interface behaves strangely, as stale cached data can sometimes interfere with dynamic results. If you're using an ad blocker or privacy extension, try temporarily disabling it, as some extensions block the API calls our search relies on. If the issue persists across browsers or devices, it may be a temporary service disruption — check our status page or community forum for announcements. You can also contact support with details about your search query and what you expected to find.

## I found incorrect information

We take accuracy seriously and appreciate your help in maintaining our data quality. If you notice incorrect information on a model page — wrong parameter count, outdated benchmarks, incorrect license, or any other factual error — please use the "Suggest Correction" button on the relevant model page. Provide as much detail as possible about what's incorrect and, ideally, a link to the authoritative source with the correct information.

Our team reviews correction suggestions regularly and verifies them against primary sources before updating our database. For urgent or critical inaccuracies (such as misattributed licensing that could affect legal use), you can also email our team directly. We aim to review and act on correction submissions within 48 hours. Contributors who consistently provide accurate corrections may be invited to join our verification community with elevated review privileges.

## How to report a bug

If you encounter a bug on LLM Trust — a broken page, unexpected behavior, display issues, or functionality that doesn't work as described — we want to hear about it. The fastest way to report a bug is through our in-app feedback tool, accessible via the "Feedback" button in the site footer. Include a description of what you were doing, what you expected to happen, and what actually happened.

For more detailed bug reports, you can also open an issue on our GitHub repository if you prefer a public, trackable format. Please include your browser and operating system version, any relevant screenshots or screen recordings, and steps to reproduce the issue. Our engineering team triages bug reports regularly, with critical issues (security, data loss, accessibility) receiving priority. You'll receive a response acknowledging your report, and we'll notify you when the fix is deployed if you've provided contact information.
